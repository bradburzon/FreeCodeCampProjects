Rapping

Samsa is a lo-fi rapper mostly known for his smooth style of rapping and crazy rhymes.

Full of beats, endless flow, and magical rhymes, Samsa will get you hooked while telling a story.

His rap songs are sung in a narrative way, giving the listeners something to visualize while listening.

Also, they are very diverse in topics which includes love, technology, religion, politics, etc.

Samsa has a Youtube Channel where his songs could be found.

Retirement

Sadly, Samsa has retired around December 2017, announcing it on his twitter. He was part of OffTheJump(OTJ) as one of its featured artists.

Many are, without a doubt, saddened about his retirement but still continue to support the artist by listening to his songs on Youtube.

Even today, many leaves comment to his work which can be full of praises but also full of saddness after knowing about his retirement.
